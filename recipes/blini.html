How to make delicious blini

Description

Blini is the delicious russian hotcake, prepare enjoy with others
Adjectives: Sweet, Soft, Cozy & Warm

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all prupose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    •

    Steps

    1. Combine flour, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
    2. Whisk 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons milk, egg, and 1 tablespoon melted butter together in a separate bowl.
      Mix into the flour mixture until the batter is fully combined.
    3. Heat 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Cooking several blini at a time, drop batter, one tablespoon at a time,
      onto the heated skillet. Cook until bubbles form, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Flip and continue cooking until brown, about 1 minute more.
      Move to a paper towel-lined plate to help soak up excess butter.
    4. Repeat with the remaining batter.
